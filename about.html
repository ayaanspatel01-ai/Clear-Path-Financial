Our story

Built for people who deserve a fair shot

ClearPath Financial was created because too many people are left behind by a financial system that wasn't designed with them in mind.

Our mission

We believe that financial stability is a right, not a privilege. Whether you're dealing with debt, recovering from past mistakes, or rebuilding after incarceration — everyone deserves clear, honest guidance and the tools to build a better future. We exist to make that possible, for free, without judgment.

Compassion first

We don't judge how you got here. We only focus on where you're going and how we can help you get there.

Radical honesty

We give you real information, not sugarcoated advice. You deserve to know the truth about your financial situation.

Community driven

Our content is built with input from the communities we serve — people who've been through it and come out the other side.

The team
