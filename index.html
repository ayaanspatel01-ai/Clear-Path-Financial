Smart money habits

For anyone struggling with debt, overspending, or simply never having been taught how to manage money well.

  • Budgeting basics and spending awareness
  • Understanding credit and how to build it

Reentry financial support

For people returning from incarceration who face additional barriers to housing, employment, and banking access.

  • Opening a bank account with a record
  • Rebuilding credit from zero

  • Securing housing with a background check
